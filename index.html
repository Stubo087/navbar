About me:

I started working in the family business at a early age, like most family businesses. This is where i got interested in the jewellery trade. I studied manufacturing and design in Manchester, along with a NVQ in gem setting, trained by a Birmingham gem setter . I also studied a National Association of Goldsmiths ,Retail jewellers Diploma . Following this working on 48 th street New York, as a gem setter for a jewellery manufacturing company. Returning home to the family business where i have being homing my skills as a crafts man ever since.

- Philip Bramley
